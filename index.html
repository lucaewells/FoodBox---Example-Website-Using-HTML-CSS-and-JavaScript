Local produce boxes for city households

FoodBox links urban homes with over 40 micro-farms using hydroponics, vertical growing and pesticide-free methods.

Main key offerings

  • Weekly boxes

    Seasonal fruit and vegetable crates delivered to the doorstep with recipe cards where included.

  • Farm tours

    Hydroponic farm visits show customers how local, low-mileage produce is grown.

  • Gardening kits

    Starter kits help families grow herbs and salad leaves in small urban spaces.

Commitment to the environment

The FoodBox platform is designed around reduced food miles, pesticide-free growing and community education. The website therefore gives quick access to box plans, crop availability, delivery scheduling and local events.
