About FoodBox

FoodBox started as a rooftop farming experiment in 2019 and has grown into a full scale agriculture network supplying fresh produce to city households.

The business collaborates with over 40 micro farms using hydroponics, vertical growing and pesticide-free practices. Our mission is to shorten the food supply chain and make sustainable eating accessible to everyone.
